HSE National Screening Service

Improving access so that everyone has access to screening regardless of who they are, where they are from, and how they define themselves (HSE).

The National Screening Service is responsible for:

  • The National Breast Screening Programme
  • The National Cervical Screening Programme
  • The National Bowel Screening Programme
  • The National Diabetic Retinal Screening Programme
